Article I: Name

SAND: Coarse Sand : Sylhet Sand (FM 2.5)
Fine Sand : Good quality fine sand free from salt.

Article II: Address

FOOTING & FOUNDATION: RCC casting of foundation will be as per structural design using ¾” downgraded crushed stone chips.
COLUMN, GRADE BEAM, RESERVOIR : RCC casting as per structural design strength using ¾” stone chips with 60 grade steel.
ROOF BEAM AND SLAB: RCC casting as per structural design strength using ¾” downgraded crushed stone/picket jhama chips(as per structural engineer recommendation)
DOOR WALL, FLASE SLAB, SHELVES etc: RCC casting as per structural design using ¾” downgraded picket jhama chips


Article III: Registration

• All external walls will be ten inches wall & internal ones will be five inches.
• Using latest testing equipment and laboratory techniques, Sub-soil Investigation and Soil Composition are comprehensively analyzed.
• Foundation and superstructure design and construction supervision done by a group of Structural Design Engineers with wide range of professional experience.
• Structural design prepared with the help of latest computer Software analysis (EXACT Method).
• Structural design is prepared considering earthquake as per BNBC-93 code and Cyclonic winds up to 210 KM}I for the structure to withstand
• All the parameters related to structural design based on American Standard of Testing Materials (ASTM) and American Concrete Institute (ACI) codes.
• Heavy reinforced cement concrete foundation depending on the soil test report
• Comprehensive section-by-section testing and checking of all steel reinforcement by Professional Designer and Supervising Engineers
• Systematic structural combination of steel reinforced concrete frame and Shear wall core
• All structural members such as columns, beams, slabs, stairs, etc. with high strength reinforced cement concrete.
• All construction materials used including cement, bricks steel sylhet sand local sand and other aggregates etc of best quality and screened properly as per specification
• To ensure highest quality of workmanship in construction direct supervision is made at every phase by a group of qualified Civil Engineers.
• Latest equipment are used for construction which includes Vibro-hammer, Welding machine, Concrete mixture machine, Concrete Vibrator, Waters pump, Leveling machine etc

Article XV: Amendment

A. Any article , clause , sub –clause or any part of the constitution, can be amended only in the AGM of two third of the forum members.
B. For any such amendment, a proposal along with its necessity is to be sent writing to the General secretary at least three weeks ahead of AGM.
C. In case of an explanation about anything not mentioned in this constitution or any confusion arises on any of its point, the council will form
a five (5) members committee to decide / clarify the issue.
